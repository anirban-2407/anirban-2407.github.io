Research Interests

  • Optics and Photonics: Nonlinear optics, spectroscopy, solar energy devices, photonic circuits, and laser-based systems.
  • Magnetic Materials and Spintronics: Experimental investigation of magnetic phenomena and spin-dependent transport in functional materials.
  • Thin Films, Two-Dimensional, and Carbon-Based Materials: Growth, characterization, and physical properties of low-dimensional material systems.
  • Energy-Efficient and Functional Materials: Materials for sustainable energy conversion, storage, and advanced technological applications.
  • Condensed Matter Experiments: Spectroscopic and physical characterization of solid-state systems.
  • High-Energy Physics Experiments: Interest in detector-based measurements and experimental probes of fundamental particles and interactions.
