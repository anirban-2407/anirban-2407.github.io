Anirban Mullick

I was born and brought up in Howrah, West Bengal, India. I completed my undergraduate studies in Physics from the University of Calcutta in 2023 and obtained my Master of Science degree from the National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli in 2025.

My academic interests lie broadly in experimental physics, with emphasis on optical and spectroscopic techniques for investigating material and environmental systems. I am particularly interested in non‑destructive diagnostics, quantitative data analysis, and interdisciplinary scientific applications of physical methods.

During my Master’s research, I studied simulated acid rain stress in Neem (Azadirachta indica) using Raman, FT‑IR, and UV‑Vis spectroscopy, focusing on identifying spectral signatures of biochemical and structural changes in plant tissues. This work strengthened my foundation in experimental design, spectral acquisition, and scientific data interpretation.

I remain open to exploring diverse directions in experimental research and aim to further develop expertise in advanced instrumentation, scientific computing, and data‑driven physical analysis.
